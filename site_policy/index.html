Use of cookies

This site REQUIRES the use of cookies. Upon signing in, a cookie is created known as your "session ID". This cookie can be linked to your account internally & verified against SPFN servers.

NOTE: due to the design of this system, we temporaily store your password to check whether it has been changed if you are using the Remember Me button.

If you do not wish to consent to this, logging out will delete it from the Database.

When storing your password, they are hashed & encrypted using the fernet key provided by the Hoster.
